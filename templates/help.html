Instructions: If a section label is incorrect or if a paragraph is too long, click to delete it. Then, select the text with your cursor and press one of the following keys to relabel it:

  • Press H for header ‐ any header or sub-header.

  • Press L for list item – short item of a bulleted or numbered text, especially an incomplete or single sentence.

  • Press P for paragraph – body paragraph text, including complete paragraphs within list items.

    • long paragraphs over {{words}} words turn red, indicating that they need to be split manually.
